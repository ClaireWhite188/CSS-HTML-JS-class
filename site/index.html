Ordered List

Making a sandwich:
  1. Place two pieces of bread on the counter.
    1. Open refrigerator.
    2. Take out bread.
    3. Open bread.
  2. Open peanut butter.
    *This can be found in the pantry.
  3. Scoop peanut butter onto the first slice of bread.
  4. Open jelly.
    *This can be found in the refrigerator.
  5. Scoop jelly onto the second slice of bread.
  6. Put slices together.
  7. Enjoy!!
Cleanup:
  1. Close peanut butter.
  2. Close jelly.
  3. Put the peanut butter in the pantry.
  4. Put the jelly in the refrigerator.
  5. Wipe counter.

What is shown above are two Ordered lists. This is a list of things that need to be completed in the exact order that it is on the list. ex.(You need to open the peanut butter before you can spread it on the bread.)
